Student Pass Prediction AI

Artificial Intelligence Project

Problem Scoping Data Acquisition Data Exploration Modeling Evaluation Deployment

1. Problem Scoping

The objective is to predict whether a student will pass or fail based on study hours, attendance, homework completion and previous marks.

Problem Statement Template

Using the 4Ws Canvas, the problem statement can be summarized as follows:

Category Description
Our (Who) Students preparing for examinations
Have a problem that (What) They are unable to predict whether they are likely to pass or need additional academic support.
When / While (Where) During their exam preparation period before the final examinations.
An ideal solution would (Why) Predict student performance using AI so that teachers and students can take timely action to improve results.

Problem Statement:

An AI-based system predicts student performance using study hours, attendance, homework completion, previous marks, and time spent online to identify students who may require additional academic support.

2. Data Acquisition

Data Acquisition is the process of collecting student information such as study hours, attendance, homework completion, previous marks, and time spent online. Accurate data helps the AI model make reliable predictions.

3. Data Exploration

The collected data is cleaned, organized, and analyzed to identify patterns. This helps the AI understand how different factors influence a student's academic performance before training the model.


Sample Student Dataset

Student Study Hours Attendance Time Online Homework Previous Marks Result

Observations

  • Students who study for more than 2 hours usually perform better.
  • Attendance above 75% increases the chances of passing.
  • Students who complete homework regularly score higher marks.
  • Previous examination marks help predict future performance.
  • Students who spend excessive time online for non-academic purposes
    may have lower academic performance due to reduced study time.
  • The cleaned dataset is then sent to the AI model for training.

4. Modeling

The trained model is tested using new student data to measure its accuracy. If the predictions are incorrect, the model is improved by retraining it with better-quality data.

Model Inputs

Input Feature Purpose
Study Hours Measures the amount of time spent studying each day.
Attendance Shows the student's regularity in attending classes.
Homework Completion Indicates whether assignments are completed regularly.
Previous Marks Provides information about past academic performance.

Prediction Logic

  • Study Hours ≥ 2 hours
  • Attendance ≥ 75%
  • Previous Marks ≥ 50
  • Homework Completed = Yes
  • Time spent online ≤ 4

If most of these conditions are satisfied, the AI predicts that the student is likely to Pass. Otherwise, it predicts that the student may Need Improvement.

5. Evaluation

The trained model is tested using new student data to measure its accuracy. If the predictions are incorrect, the model is improved by retraining it with better-quality data.

6. Deployment

After successful testing, the AI model is deployed so teachers can use it to predict student performance and identify students who may need additional academic support.
